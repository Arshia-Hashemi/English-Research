Solar energy

This mature star, which is located in the center of the solar system, transmits a lot of energy to the earth every day. 1. Solar energy is the result of nuclear fusion. Nuclear fusion is exactly the opposite of nuclear fission, in nuclear fusion, lighter nuclei like hydrogen combine and produce a heavier nucleus and a lot of energy, this is exactly what happens in the center of the sun. The energy that reaches the earth from the sun every second is much more than the consumption of the whole world. So that every second, the earth receives 174000000000000000 joules of energy from the sun and this energy reaches the earth in the form of light and heat.
