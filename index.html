Energy sources

Renewable energies: renewable energies are produced again after use, these energies often have abundant and endless sources, these energies are also called clean energies because they do not pollute the environment, in general, renewable energies are There are those whose consumption does not deplete their reserves and they are replaced after consumption. Non-renewable energies: There are energies whose consumption reduces their reserves, and at the same time, the formation of these types of resources requires long periods of time.
